Website Description

This page will describe more about this website. saying: This website was designed for the Halloween season but will provide a Halloween experience for fans of that particular holiday no matter what the time of year it may be. Visitors will be able to come to this website to read Halloween themed poetry with a holiday themed delivery perfect for the Halloween spirit. With a Halloween color scheme of green, orange, and purple, as well as themed photos are what add the Halloween holiday theme. The poetry was all chosen specifically to match the Halloween theme and are all assigned photos that describe the theme of the poem as it ties into Halloween in its own unique way. All of this combined provides the perfect environment for this specifically chosen Halloween themed poetry to be delivered. This holiday themed poetry website will answer the visitors' questions and concerns about what the best Halloween poetry is and will make it easier for them to find it. These poems range in their length, and their style to add diversity as well as to fit what each of the visitors may be looking for during the holiday season. The authors of the poems included in this Halloween themed website include: Eve Roper, Edgar Allan Poe, Adelaide Crapsey, John Keats, Walter de la Mare, James Hogg, Carl Sandburg, Heid Erdrich, and Alice Cary. The subjects of these poems range from monsters, to dark thoughts, and creepy story type narratives that are perfect for the theme of the website. Some of these poems are well known classics and others are less known works. Although, each has its unique charm as it takes a different view on the Halloween theme of the website. All of these poems are perfect for the Halloween holiday theme because of their diverse subjects and their ability to tie into together with the overall theme of the website. This website has a Halloween poem for everyone both experienced poetry readers and new poetry readers. It is difficult to find a themed website like this one. That will be the quality that will bring the interest of these different visitors as they are able to find such a specialized website. This unique theme to the website will make it stand out from the other poetry websites because of its specific theme. Other poetry websites are not themed for a specific type of poetry, for example, the Halloween themed poetry theme in this website, and will have a wider range of poetry. This website provides a specified theme making it easier for readers to find a connection between the poems and the underlying theme of Halloween. This would be more difficult for the poetry readers if they had to spend the time searching for other Halloween themed poetry. This Halloween themed website is perfect for schools introducing poetry around the Halloween season. With its holiday theme and interesting Halloween presentation, this website will provide an interesting learning experience for the students as they are able to experience several types and styles of poetry. However, no matter the context this website is the perfect place to find Halloween poetry with a perfectly holiday themed presentation. In short, this website is able to provide a themed and simple presentation of a Halloween theme as it provides the reader with poetry with that same Halloween theme as the website’s design. Each poem is unique in context, length, and in style. This provides a unique theme that cannot be found anywhere else. That will be what makes this website stand out from the other poetry websites that a poetry reader can find online on the internet.
